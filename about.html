About Us

TatisHUBconnect was launched as a resourceful online commercial service marketplace to make living more productive, convenient and efficient by outsourcing/recruiting staff for business organizations, helping more job seekers secure employment, and connecting various service providers to an unlimited market space of potential customers, geographical location notwithstanding. With the ability to leverage and harmonize both online and offline system of business backed with relevant technology, our service network spreads across the country. At TatisHUBconnect, we promote PEOPLE, POSSIBILITIES AND PERFORMANCE as our core values.

Our Corporate Vision

To remain a pioneer in the service and outsourcing industry by leveraging an automated standard online multi functional system that delivers unlimited value in connecting people to solutions of job employment and everyday services.

Our Corporate Mission

To offer outsourcing and service solutions through our online multipurpose connect platform, by efficiently providing everyday services at customers' convenience, and also bridging the recruitment gap between job seekers and employers, at various job employment levels.

To effectively and efficiently provide everyday service at customers convenience by offering outsourcing and service solution through our online multipurpose connect platform
